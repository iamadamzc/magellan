## ‚ö†Ô∏è RISKS & MITIGATIONS

### **Known Risks**

1. **Tail Risk**: Occasional -150% losses (stop loss triggered)
   - **Mitigation**: Position sizing ($10k notional per trade)
   - **Historical**: Only 1-2 catastrophic losses per 20 trades

2. **Lower Win Rate on QQQ**: 40% vs 60% on SPY
   - **Mitigation**: Smaller allocation (30% vs 70%)
   - **Observation**: Big wins compensate

3. **Margin Requirements**: Selling options requires margin
   - **Mitigation**: Ensure broker allows naked puts/calls
   - **Alternative**: Use spreads (reduces margin, also reduces profit)

4. **Market Regime Change**: Strategy needs mean reversion
   - **Mitigation**: Monitor RSI signal frequency
   - **Warning Sign**: <5 signals/year = market too trending

### **Stop Conditions**

**Exit strategy if**:
- Win rate drops below 40% for 20 trades
- Drawdown exceeds 50%
- Signal frequency drops below 5/year
- Sharpe drops below 1.0 for 6 months
